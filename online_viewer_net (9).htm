Our Story

Founded back in the 90's, Edna’s Cakeland is a Filipino bakery and café brand built on passion, craftsmanship, and the belief that food is central to every meaningful celebration.

What began as a simple home-baked loaf has grown into a trusted name in cakes, pastries, and coffee across Central Luzon.
